Hi, I'm Alexis!

I would describe myself as a collaborative, highly detail-oriented junior developer who's always seeking to find creative, user-centric solutions.

I was in the information science field for 10 years, primarily helping organizations manage their historical documents & objects and turning that into easily digestible and accessible data. When an application didn't have the features I needed, I began writing small Python scripts to work with my data. I really enjoyed the mix of creativity and logic involved in writing code and realized it was time for a change- I wanted to take on more challenge and move into a role that would enable me to make bigger impacts on the world. I decided to pivot into tech and joined a full-stack development bootcamp, where my life has become a pattern of wake up, learn, code, code, code, eat, sleep. I haven't had much time to relax, but I still feel satisfied. I think of myself as The Little Engine that Could- always be moving forwards, learning and growing along the way.

I enjoy frontend & full-stack development and still love working with data (💛SQL!). I want to use technology to help build a more sustainable future and am deeply interested in working in the climate/ESG tech space as a developer or data analyst/engineer.

I'm really excited to collaborate on a team and apply my new technical skills to develop robust applications that positively impact the world. If any of this resonates with you, contact me!
